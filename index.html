A HiFi project by Benjamin Uribe and Owen Caldwell
Made for Analog Heaven @ New York Unversity
November - December 2024M

Analog Heaven is a course within the Integrated Design & Media program at NYU Tandon’s School of Engineering that offers undergraduate students the opportunity to learn analog electrical engineering, sound theory, and experimental audio. Throughout the semester, our curriculum focused on introductory circuit building, how-to’s in reading schematics, and experimental synth operation– all of which builds up to a end of semester case of student's final works.

Introduction

Holes cut for left and right speaker connections on the back

As a two person operation, our final project focused on two components– a speaker build and a 1st Order Butterworth Passive Crossover. Throughout this project experience, we made strides in understanding the relationship between Speaker drivers, the amp that drives them, and frequency manipulation through a crossover attachment.
